Who Am I

NEEL

My name is Swapneel Das, though most people know me as NEEL.

I am a 21-year-old student from Sunamganj, Sylhet, Bangladesh, currently pursuing a Bachelor's (Honours) in English Literature & Language at the National University of Bangladesh.

I am naturally quiet and introverted. I don't seek attention or validation from others. I believe real growth happens in silence, through discipline, patience, and consistency. While many people chase quick results, I prefer building skills that last a lifetime.

My Mission

My biggest goal is to achieve financial freedom through trading. Trading is not just a source of income for me—it's the path I've chosen to build my future. Every chart I study, every mistake I learn from, and every improvement I make brings me one step closer to becoming consistently profitable.

My Trading Journey

My primary market is XAU/USD (Gold). After exploring different markets and concepts, I developed my own trading approach that fits my understanding and personality.

My analysis focuses on:

  • Higher-timeframe bias
  • Market structure
  • Liquidity
  • Supply and Demand
  • QML
  • RBS/SBR
  • CHOCH
  • Fibonacci retracements
  • Confirmation before execution
I believe capital preservation comes before profit. I'd rather miss an opportunity than take a trade that doesn't meet my rules.

My Routine

My day revolves around the London and New York trading sessions. I prepare before the market opens, execute only when my conditions are met, and avoid unnecessary trades. Consistency and discipline are the foundations of my trading journey.

My Mindset

One belief guides almost everything I do:

"It is easier to find another entry than another capital."

Patience is my edge. I don't measure success by how many trades I take—I measure it by how well I follow my plan.

I am building my trading identity under one name:

NEELX PIPS

It represents my journey as a trader, my commitment to continuous learning, and my vision of building a community where discipline, education, and consistency matter more than hype or shortcuts.

My Dream

One of my biggest dreams is to own a Porsche 911 before I turn 30.

To me, it isn't just a luxury car—it's a symbol of years of discipline, sacrifice, patience, and consistent execution. I don't dream about overnight success; I dream about earning it.
